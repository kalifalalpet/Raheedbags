Travel Tips & Guides

Advice from luggage experts

How to choose the right luggage

Consider trip length, transportation type, and what you're packing. Hard cases offer protection, soft bags offer flexibility.

Cabin luggage size guide

Standard international cabin size is typically 55 x 35 x 20 cm, ensuring your bag complies with most airlines to avoid extra fees.

Hard case vs Soft luggage

Polycarbonate hard shells protect fragile items from impacts, while nylon soft sides allow you to stuff that extra jacket.
